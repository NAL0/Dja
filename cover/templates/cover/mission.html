New Age Liberty

New Age liberty is an informal non-profit organisation created to achieve the goal of African unity in our lifetime. Our vision is of a prosperous and united Africa without colonial boarders driven by the spirit of Ubuntu.

If you love Africa and share our vision of a united Africa then help us by donating your time in our voluteer program. If you have no time to spare a donation from your wallet will be helpful as well.
